Recover Your AVG Account Password
---------------------------------

If you've forgotten your password, don’t panic. AVG offers a simple recovery process:

1. Visit the `AVG password reset page <https://myaccount.avg.com/forgot-password>`_
2. Enter the email associated with your AVG account.
3. Click **Continue**.
4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
5. Follow the link and create a new password.

**Note:** The reset link is valid for only a limited time. If it expires, repeat the process.

Can’t Find the Reset Email?

- Make sure you're using the correct email address tied to your AVG subscription.
- If still missing, try resending the password reset link after a few minutes.

Common AVG Login Issues and How to Fix Them
-------------------------------------------

### 1. Invalid Login Credentials

One of the most frequent issues is entering the wrong email or password.

**Fix:**
- Double-check for typos.
- Make sure Caps Lock isn’t turned on.
- If you’re unsure, use the password reset link.

### 2. Account Locked After Multiple Failed Attempts

Too many failed login attempts can temporarily lock your AVG account.

**Fix:**
- Wait at least 15 minutes before trying again.
- Use the **Forgot Password** option to reset your credentials.
- If you continue having problems, contact `AVG support <https://support.avg.com>`_.

### 3. Not Receiving Password Reset Emails

This often happens when users mistype their email or the email is delayed.

**Fix:**
- Confirm you're using the correct email.
- Check all folders (spam, promotions, etc.)
- Add AVG’s email to your whitelist or safe sender list.

### 4. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Issues

If you've enabled 2FA and lost access to your authentication method, you may be locked out.

**Fix:**
- Use your backup codes if available.
- If you’ve lost both your device and backup codes, reach out to `AVG customer support <https://support.avg.com>`_ for manual recovery.
